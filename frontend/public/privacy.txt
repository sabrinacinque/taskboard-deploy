PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 2025

1. INTRODUCTION
Welcome to TaskBoard. This Privacy Policy explains how TaskBoard (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our web application. By accessing or using TaskBoard, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
2.1. Personal Information You Provide Directly:  
     • Username  
     • Email address  
     • Phone number  
     • Password (encrypted)  
     • Any other information you choose to provide (e.g., profile settings, preferences)
2.2. Usage Data Collected Automatically:  
     • IP address  
     • Browser type and version  
     • Device information (such as device model, operating system)  
     • Pages visited and time spent on pages  
     • Referring/exit pages  
     • Date and time of access  
     • Clickstream data (buttons clicked, features used)
2.3. Cookies and Similar Technologies:  
     • We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to track login sessions, preferences, and usage patterns.  
     • Cookies are small text files placed on your device. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though disabling cookies may affect some features of TaskBoard.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
3.1. To Provide and Maintain TaskBoard:  
     • Create and manage your account  
     • Authenticate and authorize your access  
     • Store and retrieve your tasks, projects, and other data  
3.2. To Improve User Experience:  
     • Analyze usage data to understand feature adoption and performance  
     • Optimize the application, fix bugs, and enhance security  
3.3. To Communicate with You:  
     • Send you account-related notifications (e.g., password resets, login alerts)  
     • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries  
     • Send promotional materials or newsletters (you may opt out at any time)
3.4. To Enforce Legal Rights and Fulfill Legal Obligations:  
     • Comply with applicable laws, regulations, or court orders  
     • Protect our rights and property, as well as the rights and property of others

4. HOW WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION
4.1. Service Providers and Third-Party Partners:  
     • We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, email delivery). These providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only to perform services for TaskBoard.  
4.2. Legal Obligations and Protection:  
     • We may disclose your information if required by law, subpoena, or court order, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or those of our users or the public.  
4.3. With Your Consent:  
     • We may share personal information with other parties when you give us explicit consent to do so.

5. DATA RETENTION
5.1. We retain your personal information and User Content for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.  
5.2. If you delete your account, we will remove your User Content from public view. However, certain data may be retained in backup or archived files for a reasonable time to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and for legitimate business purposes.

6. SECURITY MEASURES
6.1. We implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.  
6.2. Despite our efforts, no security measure is completely infallible. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

7. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES
7.1. Access and Correction:  
     • You may access or update certain information in your account settings. If you need assistance, contact us at privacy@taskboard.com.  
7.2. Deletion:  
     • You can request deletion of your account, which will remove your data from active systems. Some data may remain in backups or as required by law.  
7.3. Opt-Out of Marketing Communications:  
     • If you no longer wish to receive promotional emails or newsletters, follow the “unsubscribe” link in any email or contact us at privacy@taskboard.com.  
7.4. Cookies:  
     • You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may impact certain features.

10. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS
TaskBoard is hosted on servers that may be located in multiple countries. When you use our service, your data may be transferred to and stored on servers outside your country of residence. By using TaskBoard, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

11. CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY
